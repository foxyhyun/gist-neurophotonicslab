Research Field

In the neurophotonics lab, I am studying Alzheimer's disease (AD) development and pathophysiology. My research expertise is in vascular biology, and therefore, I am trying to study the vascular defects in mice's brain during AD as well as their neuronal and cognitive loss. For this research, I will use two-photon imaging to capture live capillary blood flow in the brain, confocal imaging of brain tissues for vascular examination, and different methods and techniques to study various behavioral tests for AD mice.
